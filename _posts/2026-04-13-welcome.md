This blog is where I document what works, what breaks, and what changes my mind while building software.

I've noticed that the best lessons disappear if I don't capture them quickly. A sharp bug fix, a design tradeoff, a surprising production incident — they all feel obvious in the moment and fuzzy a week later.

So this is my running notebook. Short, practical entries with enough detail to be useful when I need to revisit the same problem again.
